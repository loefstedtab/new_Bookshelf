Blakely Art

www.ArtPal.com/mattieblakely

Thank you so much for your interest in my artwork! My name is Mattie, I'm a wife and mother of 5 currently living in Missouri. I've been painting for about 5 years now, and while I love arts and crafts of every kind, my passion is painting. My medium of choice is typically acrylics, but have been known to dabble in watercolors from time to time. I prefer to paint historical places and landmarks, landscapes and water scenes. I particularly love painting scenes from Scotland.
